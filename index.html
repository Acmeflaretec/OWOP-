Our Missions

Welcome to OWOP International Foundation, where our dedication knows no bounds and our mission is clear: to ignite positive change and transform lives worldwide.

Mitra Project

The Mitra Project is a visionary initiative by ONE WORLD ONE PEOPLE that centers around building impactful friendships to catalyze positive change within local communities.

BHAVAN Project

The 'BHAVAN' project by One World One People provides vital shelter to those in need. Through dedicated volunteers, generous donors, and skilled professionals, we bring hope and transformation to communities worldwide.

Hope Project

Welcome to Name of Hope's Widow and Orphan Sponsorship Project! We're dedicated to providing support and opportunities to widows and orphans facing adversity. Through our sponsorship program, we offer education, healthcare, and livelihood assistance, empowering them for a brighter future.

FILL the plate Project

Welcome to 'FILL the plate,' our mission is clear: to eradicate hunger and bring hope to those in need. Through partnerships and compassionate support, we provide nutritious meals to vulnerable communities, addressing the root causes of hunger. Join us in creating a world where every plate is filled with love and opportunity.

Future Project

Welcome to 'Future,' where we empower the next generation through education. With scholarships, resources, and mentorship, we ensure every child has access to quality education. Join us in breaking the cycle of poverty and creating a brighter tomorrow. Together, we can build a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive.

HUG Project

Welcome to 'HUG,' where we fulfill the dreams of every parent by providing gold jewelry to daughters on their wedding day. We support girls from impoverished backgrounds, ensuring they have a dignified wedding experience. Join us in spreading love and joy, empowering these deserving young women to embark on their new journey with hope and pride

KLOTH Project

Welcome to 'KLOTH,' where we provide clothing to those in need with dignity and warmth. With your support, we distribute new or gently used clothes, uplifting spirits and restoring pride. Join us in making a tangible difference, one person at a time, as we weave a tapestry of warmth, comfort, and dignity for all.

NUTRIFOOD Project

Welcome to 'NUTRIFOOD,' where we believe in providing nutritious food for all. With your support, we address hunger and malnutrition in our community by providing nourishing meals to those in need. Join us in making a difference today! Together, we can nourish the bodies and souls of those who need it the most.

PURE Project

Welcome to 'PURE,' where we're dedicated to restoring water bodies and providing clean water sources to communities in need. With your support, we construct wells, ponds, and handpumps, ensuring sustainable access to this essential resource. Join us in creating a world where every drop of water brings hope and prosperity. Together, we can make a ripple of change, one water source at a time.

SAATH Project

Welcome to 'SAATH,' where we offer a lifeline to those in extreme poverty burdened by debt and despair. With tailored solutions, financial aid, and counseling services, we empower individuals to overcome adversity and regain control of their lives. Join us in bringing hope and renewed purpose to those in need. Together, we can make a profound impact and remind every individual that they are not alone.
